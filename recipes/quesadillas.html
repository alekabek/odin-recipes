Quesadillas

  • Place your ground beef into the preheated pan. After you break it apart, add some salt, cumin and pepper
  • Add some minced onions and peppers
  • Blend your cauliflower in the food processor
  • When the beef is 80% cooked, add some minced garlic. Mix everything and add cauliflower
  • Add the rest of your seasonings and mix everything well
  • On a separate pan start heating up a tortilla on low heat.
  • Add some cheese to tortilla and carefully place your meat on top. Add more cheese
  • Place another tortilla on top and gently press everything together
  • When the cheeses are nicely melted, and bottom tortilla is slightly crispy, flip your quesadilla and wait for the other tortilla to crispen up
  • Voila! Your quesadilla is ready to be served
